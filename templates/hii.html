Electricity Bill Prediction

This project aims to predict the electricity bill for a given time period using historical data and machine learning algorithms. By analyzing past consumption patterns, weather conditions, and other relevant parameters, the model will generate accurate predictions to help users plan their energy usage and budget accordingly. The predictions can be used by individuals, households, or businesses to optimize their electricity consumption and reduce costs.
